My Favourite food Recipe : Chilli Paneer

Ingredients

For the Dough :

  1. 1pcs Onion
  2. 2pcs Capsicum
  3. 1pcs Garam Masala
  4. 1pcs Turmeric Masala
  5. 1pcs Red Chilli Masala

For The Filling :

  1. 1 packet Oil
  2. 200gm Paneer

Instructions

  1. Boil the oil for 3 minutes.
  2. Put the onion in oil.
  3. Add green chilli and onion.
  4. Mix Capsicum and Paneer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add masala in these items
  6. Enjoy it.
